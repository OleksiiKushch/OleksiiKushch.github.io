Project Description

Continuous delivery initiative for CTC Digital

Multiple campaign websites (marketing banners) with individual domains that are unified under under a centralized Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) platform, enabling streamlined management and showcasing the fully leveraged strengths and capabilities of the technology.

Responsibilities

  • Develop new features and maintain (update and fix bugs) existing functionality;
  • Create documentation for AEM authors (for release);
  • Review pull requests and provide feedback;
  • Work on technical debt: increase test coverage, refactor existing code;
  • Collaborate with the team (daily meetings, progress updates, troubleshooting, knowledge sharing and so on) and maintain regular communication with the client about my current task status;

Achievements

  • Extended and maintained AEM Content Fragments and Components, including adding new properties for CF and new fields in dialogs, as well as updating or removing existing ones;
  • Contributed to the implementation and troubleshooting of SSO (Single Sign-On) and SSSO (Simplified SSO with One-Time Password) authentication flows;
  • Worked on AEM page properties (configuration) and dispatcher redirect rules;
